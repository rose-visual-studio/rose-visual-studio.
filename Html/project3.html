Chicken Alfredo

serving size:4

    Ingrediant List
  • 4 piece- boneless chicken breast
  • 2 box- 16oz.penne pasta
  • 1package- 16oz.Diced Ham
  • 1 package- 11z. freash spinach
  • 1 block- 16oz. mozzaralla cheese
  • 1 block- 16oz. sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cans- 32oz.each cream of chicken
  • 4 tablespoons- butter
  • 2 tablespoons- olive oil
  • buttery steakhouse seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1- clove of garlic
  • 3-4 toothpicks per piece of chicken

fillet chicken thinly. place handful of shredded mozzeralla, diced ham, and spinach on filleted chicken. roll chicken up and place 3 to 4 toothpicks through the chicken to keep it closed. season the outside with salt, pepper,buttery steakhouse seasoning heat on high butter oil and garlic in skillet until it butter is melted and hot. Place chicken in skillet sear both sides of chicken until dark brown. remove chicken place chicken in the oven on 350 degrees for 30 minutes make sure to cover with foil and place butter in pan use the amount you fill comfortable with. boil penne pasta,while making sauce. In skillet mix cream of chicken shredded mozzeralla, shredded cheddar cheese, melt it all together. Then to sauce add salt, pepper, buttery steakhouse seasoning to your liking.place diced ham and spinach. Cook until tender spinach. drain pasta mix together and remove toothpicks, slice chicken and top with chicken. Enjoy!

Chicken Rice

serving size: 4

    Ingrediant List
  • 4- boneless chicken breast
  • 1 package- rice
  • 2- zucchini
  • 2- gloves of garlic
  • 1cup- pico de gallo
  • 2stocks- broccoli
  • 1 half bag- baby carrots
  • 2- eggplants
  • 1- 16oz. package smoked Applewood cheddar cheese
  • 2 teaspoon- liquid smoke
  • 2 teaspoons- red wine vinegar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • hot honey seasoning
  • olive oil
  • butter

bring water for rice to boil, add rice stir, cover rice and put on simmer. Heat olive oil, spoon full of butter, and diced garlic. place chicken in skillet sprinkle hot honey seasoning evenly over it salt and pepper to taste. put 2 tablespoons each of red wine vinager and liquid smoke on chicken once flip chicken and repeat. blackend chicken while dicing zucchini, eggplants, broccli, and carrots. slice block of cheese top chicken and cover placing on simmer to finish cooking and steaming cheese to melt. once done remove from skillet and cover. add butter and oil to the skillet heat up add vegtables. Top with red wine vinager, liquid smoke, and hot honey seasoning. cook until tender. plate rice, vegtables, chicken, and picco de gallo. Enjoy!

Chicken Spaghetti

serving size:4

    Ingrediant List
  • 4- boneless chicken breasts
  • spaghetti
  • 1- bag of cube mild cheddar cheese
  • 1- 2lb 13oz jar prego freash mushroom sauce
  • 1- 4 oz package Applewood Smoked cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons- red wine vinager
  • 2 tablespoons- liquid smoke
  • tequila lime seasoning
  • italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • chica licka bam bam bird rub
  • 2 tablespoons- sugar
  • butter
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • parmesan and romano cheese

Heat oil and butter in skillet place chicken breast in skillet season with chica licka bam bam bird rub, tequila lime seasoning, fry until done sit aside. cook spaghetti noodles according to package. make sauce in skillet place sauce, red wine vinager, liquid smoke, chica licka bam bam bird rub, tequila lime seasoning, italian seasoning, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, sugar, Applewood smoked cheddar cheese, cube cheese, and shredded chicken. bring to a boil make sure cheese is melted. drain noodles plate noodles then sauce and top with parmesan and romano cheese. Enjoy!
